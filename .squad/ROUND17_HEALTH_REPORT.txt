## LATENT FIX DETAILS

**Problem:** Keaton-r16 migrated motion_view from `Position+Velocity` to `WorldTransform+MotionVelocity`.
This exposed a latent path: freeplay LowBar/HighBar (no BeatInfo, has WorldTransform+MotionVelocity+ObstacleScrollZ)
now matched both scroll_system's model_view AND motion_system's motion_view, risking double-integration:
- scroll_system: oz.z += vel.y*dt; wt->position.y = oz.z
- motion_system: wt->position.y += vel.y*dt  ← wrong!

**Solution (r17):** Option A — Structural Exclusion
  motion_view now excludes ObstacleScrollZ: `entt::exclude<BeatInfo, ObstacleScrollZ>`
  Result: scroll_system and motion_system views are mutually exclusive on the discriminator component.
  All production paths match exactly one integrator.

**Verification:**
  - Fail-then-fix test: pre-fix produced 200.0f (2× integration); post-fix passes (150.0f single integration)
  - Archetype table verified: all entity types route correctly to exactly one integrator
  - No regressions: all 18 modules green
