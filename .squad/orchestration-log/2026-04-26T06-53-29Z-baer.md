### Regression Mitigations

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Bars at easy/medium | Medium | `DIFFICULTY_KINDS` excludes; `--difficulty all` flag detects |
| `lane_of()` returns center (1) for bars | Low | Intentional lane-reset; sequence natural |
| High bar density making hard unplayable | Low | Variety threshold max_consecutive=2 limits runs |
| Beat scheduler spawns bar without lane | None | Hardcodes `LANE_X[1]`; no lane field needed |
